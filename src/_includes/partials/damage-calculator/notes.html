Full Damage Formula

(ActionMultiplier × BaseAttack + ExtraDamage) × CritDmg × (0.95~1.05) × AnyOtherMultipliers

Important

This calculator only covers damage multipliers; it does not include attack multipliers. This is done to produce a percentage total rather a number total. Your true damage is also influenced by other things like attack multipliers and DoT.

Other Notes

Effects that activate “when entering combat” are not included. Zero’s 4 set not included because it only lasts 4 seconds. {{ characters.claudia.name }}’s 4 set not included because it’s only for combo skills.
{{ characters.crow.name }} & {{ characters.shiro.name }}’s chip sets are calculated assuming you will fight an enemy from 100% HP to 0%.
KING’s 2 set can be stacked by activating it on different weapons.

Additional buffs should be multiplied together and inputted in Any other multipliers.
Ex: To calculate two buffs, 30% and 15%, do 1.30 × 1.15 to get 1.495. Then input 49.5% to get your result.
